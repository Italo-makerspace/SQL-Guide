A Practical Guide to SQL

Welcome to our practical guide to SQL. We, Leonardo, Guilherme, Thierry, and Maria Isabella, have created this website with a clear objective: to demystify the process of learning about databases.

Here, you will find accessible material, ideal for both beginners taking their first steps and professionals looking to sharpen their skills. Please navigate through the sections to explore the cornerstones of SQL, including DDL, DML, and DCL commands, as well as Aggregate Functions, Data Types, and more.
